We respect your right to delete your account and data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the RhemaFlow app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (tap the profile icon in the top right)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your user profile (name, email, photo)
  • All saved declarations and favorites
  • Usage history and preferences
  • Notification settings
  • All generated content associated with your account
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Timeframe

Account deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation. All associated data is removed from our systems right away.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions. Please cancel any subscriptions through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before deleting your account.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at support@rhemaflow.com.
